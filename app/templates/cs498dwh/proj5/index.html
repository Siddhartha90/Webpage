Image Stitching


Semi-Automated Stitching Point correspondences are first established manually using 'ginput' command. (I used 6 points) The 'Normalized Direct Linear Transform' method is used then to compute the Homography matrix, which contains 12 degrees of freedom corresponding to the 6 x,y points. The second image is then warped with the computed Homography transformation matrix thus obtained and merged with the first image.
