Computational Photography Projects

-Siddhartha gupta


We use principles of computer vision and graphics to come up with more visually appealing and life-like imagery. We create optical illusions, add or remove objects from a photograph, smoothly morph between faces, automatically stitch together photos into panoramas, enhance colors and intensities of images, merge one into another, and so much more!
