Embedded Systems

One of my favorite projects, ball balancing on a touchscreen board through low level C programming to interact with a microcontroller (dsPIC33f). Position was set through a joystick, and PID Control enabled the ball to move into the location. PID Control is:

Proportional - This is what drives the ball towards its destination coordinates.
Integral - If the ball does get stuck at a point, Integral (sum of instantaneous error over time) builds up and gives the ball a jump.
Derivative - This decelerates the ball as it approaches its destination, thus preventing from overshooting too far and eventually the ball converges to its destination.



The hours spent in lab were rewarding, and especially helped me learn how to work with long complicated datasheets (My previous experience with datasheets was limited to a bit with Arduino and i8259 PIC).
